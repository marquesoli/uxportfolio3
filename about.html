Hi! I'm Anderson, but call me Andy.

I am a Product Designer from the vibrant northeast region of Brazil, now living in Vancouver, Canada. I enjoy creating user-centric experiences for the modern human being.

My journey started exploring branding and print, then moved into digital media, which helped me transition to web design and user experience design - where I discovered one of my true passions.

I graduated from Universidade Tiradentes with a degree in Graphic Design. I loved the user-centered design methodologies I learned in school, and I constantly strive to evolve and keep learning more. I specialized in Mobile Development at PUC Minas and in Web and Mobile Design and Development at Langara College.

My interest in art and design began early, as I used to draw and paint frequently. Over time, I developed a strong interest in technology, leading me to realize that I could create visual experiences that positively impact people's lives through design.

I take inspiration from everything around me, whether it's street sign typography or a simple receipt from my favorite coffee shop. Embracing diverse cultures and empathizing with people's different perspectives is also a key part of my design process.
